The [Woodward and Bernstein papers](http://www.hrc.utexas.edu/exhibitions/online/woodstein/).

Woodward and Berstein of the Washington Post wrote a series of stories leading to the resignation of Richard Nixon. Their novel [All The President's Men](http://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/tg/detail/-/0671894412/?tag=sanand-20) has a gripping account of their adventure. Now, their original papers are available at the University of Texas.
