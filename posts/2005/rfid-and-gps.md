- [cars](http://yro.slashdot.org/article.pl?sid=05/02/15/201217&tid=158&tid=219),
- [trucks](http://yro.slashdot.org/article.pl?sid=04/12/30/1623208&tid=158&tid=126),

- [driver's licenses](http://yro.slashdot.org/article.pl?sid=04/10/08/1636231&tid=103&tid=158),
- [ID cards](http://yro.slashdot.org/article.pl?sid=04/08/08/1711239&tid=158&tid=17),
- [loyalty cards](http://yro.slashdot.org/article.pl?sid=04/02/12/166232&tid=158&tid=187&tid=17),

- [rifles](http://science.slashdot.org/article.pl?sid=04/04/12/1745241&tid=158&tid=126&tid=14),
- [casino chips](http://yro.slashdot.org/article.pl?sid=04/01/09/1828225&tid=158&tid=98&tid=17),
- [shopping carts](http://yro.slashdot.org/article.pl?sid=03/10/30/1735225&tid=158&tid=126),
- [soda cans](http://yro.slashdot.org/article.pl?sid=03/09/30/189208&tid=126&tid=158),
