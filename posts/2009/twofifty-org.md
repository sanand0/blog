It's been a good movie month for me, and I've managed to nudge closer to my target of watching the [IMDb Top 250](http://www.imdb.com/chart/top).
But one tool I had in the past, that I sorely miss, is **twofifty.org**. It's a now-defunct site that kept track of the [IMDb Top 250](http://www.imdb.com/chart/top), and let you strike off the movies that you had watched. You could see which movies you hadn't seen, keep score, and discuss the movies.
Since it's demise, my movie watching slowed down as well.
Earlier this month, I set up a similar site at [250.s-anand.net](http://250.s-anand.net/). It has the same basic function. You can log in, strike out movies that you've seen, and keep track of what's left to see. For the more technically minded, the source-code is at [two-fifty.googlecode.com](http://two-fifty.googlecode.com/).

[Visit 250.s-anand.net](http://250.s-anand.net/)

[Happy movie tracking](http://250.s-anand.net/), and looking forward to your suggestions.
