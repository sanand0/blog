Can foreigners break into #Hollywood?

A break into films is hard. Particularly when you're a foreigner. But is Hollywood more open or less open than other countries?

If we go by iconic actors, the numbers are not encouraging. Just 2% of Will Smith's co-stars are non-Hollywood. But over 30% of Jackie Chan's co-stars are non-Chinese.

But #clustering shows that Hollywood is actually among the most open to foreigners. #dataanalysis
