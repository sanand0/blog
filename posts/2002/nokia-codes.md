<p>Nokia codes (works on my 3310)</p>
<table class="lines">
<tr><td>*#war0anty#     </td><td>Warranty details</td></tr>
<tr><td>*3370#  </td><td>Better sound, drains battery</td></tr>
<tr><td>#3370#  </td><td>Deactivates above</td></tr>
<tr><td>*#0000#         </td><td>Software version</td></tr>
<tr><td>*#06#   </td><td>IMEI number</td></tr>
<tr><td>*#sim0clock#    </td><td>Allow simclock stoppage</td></tr>
<tr><td>*3001#12345#    </td><td>Enables field test mode, other options</td></tr>
<tr><td>*#92702689#     </td><td>System menu</td></tr>
</table>
<p>BPL Mobile Call Divert options</p>
<table class="lines">

<tr><td>**62*700#       </td><td>If number is unreachable</td></tr>
<tr><td>**61*700nn#     </td><td>If no reply for nn seconds</td></tr>
<tr><td>**21*700#       </td><td>All calls</td></tr>
<tr><td>##002#  </td><td>Cancel all call diverts</td></tr>
