The [Oracle of Google](http://www.mycgiserver.com/~Morat/) is the coolest Google app so far. Ask it a question with 4 choices, and it uses Google to guess the answer. It knows that [Abdul Kalam is India's President](http://www.mycgiserver.com/servlet/Morat.GoogleTest?question=Who+is+India%27s+President%3F&answers=R+Venkatraman&answers=Abdul+Kalam&answers=Shankar+Dayal+Sharma&answers=Vajpayee), though it did [prefer Vajpayee to former Presidents](http://www.mycgiserver.com/servlet/Morat.GoogleTest?question=Who+is+India%27s+President%3F&answers=R+Venkatraman&answers=S+Radhakrishnan&answers=Shankar+Dayal+Sharma&answers=Vajpayee). [via Google Weblog](http://google.blogspace.com/)
