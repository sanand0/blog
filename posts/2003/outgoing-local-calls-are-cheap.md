The cost of an outgoing local call (at least, as of today) is:\
Rs 0.40 for landline-landline\
Rs 0.80 for landline-WLL\
Rs 1.20 for landline-cell\
Rs 1.75 for cell-cell\
Rs 1.99 for cell-landline\
