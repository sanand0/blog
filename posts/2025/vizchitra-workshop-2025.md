We created data visualizations _just_ using LLMs at my VizChitra workshop yesterday.

Titled **Prompt to Plot**, it covered:

- Finding a dataset
- Ideating what to do with it
- Analyzing the data
- Visualizing the data
- Publishing it on GitHub

... using _only_ LLM tools like #ChatGPT, #Claude, #Jules, #Codex, etc. with zero manual coding, analysis, or story writing.

Here're 6 stories completed _during_ the 3-hour workshop:

- Spotify Data Stories: https://rishabhmakes.github.io/llm-dataviz/
- The Price of Perfection: https://coffee-reviews.prayashm.com/
- The Anatomy of Unrest: https://story-b0f1c.web.app/
- The Page Turner's Paradox: https://devanshikat.github.io/BooksVis/
- Do Readers Love Long Books? https://nchandrasekharr.github.io/booksviz/
- Books Viz: https://rasagy.in/books-viz/

The material is online. Try it!
