[Jugglezine](http://www.jugglezine.com/): How to find the time to do anything (not everything!)

> On a purely practical level, however, the reason we can't find time for that one thing we've always wanted to do is that we don't set aside time for it.

> Logging how much time things take for two weeks will transform you, because if you know and acknowledge in advance how long something will take, then you can make wise decisions about where to spend your time
