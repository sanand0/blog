Two MIT students have written a 7-line program that [unscrambles protected DVDs](http://www.zdnet.com/zdnn/stories/news/0,4586,2693768,00.html). It's [downloadable](http://www.cs.cmu.edu/~dst/DeCSS/Gallery/). The legal issue is whether source code deserves protection on par with freedom of expression.
