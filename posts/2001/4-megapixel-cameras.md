The 4.1 mega pixel cameras have come in: Olympus' [C4040](http://www.digital-camerastore.com/olympusc4040.htm), Toshiba's [PDR M81](http://www.toshiba.com/taisisd/dsc/products/cameras/pdrm81/index.htm), Casio's [QV4000](http://www.casio.com/cameras/product.cfm?section=176), Sony's [DSC S85](http://www.digital-camerastore.com/sonys85.htm) and Canon's [G2](http://www.digital-camerastore.com/canong2.htm).
