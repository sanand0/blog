Fashion lessons paid off. After reading about [business wardrobes](http://salesdoctors.com/surgery/4ward1.htm) for men, and how [gentlemen dress](http://www.studio267.com/guide.html), I [shopped for a suit](http://www.learn2.com/04/0420/04206.asp), hoping to look my [professional best](http://www.ext.msstate.edu/pubs/pub1974.htm). Ended up with a grey single-breasted polywool suit from Louise Phillipe.
