After seeing [how costly it is to detect hacking](http://www.zdnet.com/zdnn/stories/news/0,4586,5079958,00.html), and the fact that anything you say can be [logged against you](http://news.cnet.com/news/0-1005-200-5148422.html), one wonders if being online is worth it. If you've got a permanent Internet connection, keep it switched off.
