[George Gilder](http://www.gildertech.com) proposes that while Moore's law drove the IT revolution through processing power, today it's bandwidth that's driving it -- through [Gilder's Law](http://www.csee.uq.edu.au/~cs270/Articles/Gilder.13.11.99.htm). This ties back to what an Economist survey says about focus shifting from [software to online services](http://www.economist.com/surveys/PrinterFriendly.cfm?Story_ID=568249).
