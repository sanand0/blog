There's a lot you can do using e-mail, including [upload FTP files](http://mail2ftp.hypermart.net/), [create home pages](http://inetw.com), [send snail-mail](mailto:info@papermail.win-uk.net), [translate languages](http://www.t-mail.com), [play games](mailto:win@yoyo.com) (blank e-mail), [send a fax](mailto:tpcfaq@info.tpc.int) (no subject, "help" in body), [track webpage changes](http://www.web2mail.com/subscribe.php), etc. The full details are available at the [Accessing the Internet by Email FAQ](http://www.faqs.org/faqs/internet-services/access-via-email/)
