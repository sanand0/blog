The [Ancient History Sourcebook](http://www.fordham.edu/halsall/ancient/asbook.html) contains many ancient books in full-text (English), such as The Odyssey, The Code of Hammurabi, etc. Also included are the [Manusmriti](http://www.fordham.edu/halsall/india/manu-full.html) and Chanakya's [Arthashastra](http://www.fordham.edu/halsall/india/kautilya1.html). Chanakya has also written a lesser known [Niti Shastra](http://www.philosophy.ru/library/asiatica/indica/authors/kautilya/canakya_niti_sastra.html).
