While in the US services sector [employment is falling](http://www.timesofindia.com/070601/07inte9.htm), high tech employment, which is about 8% of the services sector, [continues to grow](http://www.msnbc.com/news/583499.asp). Meaning technology is still growing, despite the recession. Strange!
