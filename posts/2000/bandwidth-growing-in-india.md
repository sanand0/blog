Looks like India's bandwidth problems will be resolved in a year. With the DoT opening up [submarine fiber optic](http://india.internet.com/news/article/0,1942,1281_393681,00.html), Department of Telecommunication Services is [commissioning](http://www.economictimes.com/today/26infr01.htm) [80GB](http://www.economictimes.com/today/26tech03.htm) (2001). [NASSCOM](http://www.nasscom.org/)'s getting [100GB](http://india.internet.com/news/article/0,1942,1281_367011,00.html) (2003). [Dishnet](http://www.dishnetdsl.com/)'s trying for [2.5TB](http://india.internet.com/news/article/0,1942,1281_407201,00.html) (2001). Wow!
