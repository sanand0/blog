India's truly on the Net. With a slew of localized sites -- [Yahoo! India](http://in.yahoo.com), [CNet India](http://india.cnet.com/), [India.internet.com](http://india.internet.com), and [ZDNet India](http://www.zdnetindia.com/). -- we've arrived! Don't underestimate any of them, especially Yahoo! and its [finance](http://in.finance.yahoo.com/), [news](http://in.news.yahoo.com/) (with [full coverage](http://fullcoverage.yahoo.com/fc/World/India/)) and [movie](http://in.movies.yahoo.com/) sites.
