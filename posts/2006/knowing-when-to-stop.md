[Mathematics, marriage and finding somewhere to eat](http://plus.maths.org/issue3/marriage/index.html) has a simple solution to all these problems. Whether you're hiring someone, or picking a partner, or finding a house -- or any problem that requires you to pick the best among N choices -- here's the rule.

> Scan the first 37% of choices. Then pick the first one that's better than anything you've seen so far.
