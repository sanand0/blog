Jeff proposes a [Programmer's Bill of Rights](http://www.codinghorror.com/blog/archives/000666.html).

- Every programmer shall have [two monitors](http://www.codinghorror.com/blog/archives/000012.html)
- Every programmer shall have a [fast PC](http://www.codinghorror.com/blog/archives/000029.html)
- Every programmer shall have their choice of [mouse](http://www.codinghorror.com/blog/archives/000286.html) and [keyboard](http://www.codinghorror.com/blog/archives/000209.html)
- Every programmer shall have a [comfortable chair](http://www.codinghorror.com/blog/archives/000240.html)
- Every programmer shall have a fast internet connection
- Every programmer shall have [quiet working conditions](http://www.codinghorror.com/blog/archives/000154.html)
