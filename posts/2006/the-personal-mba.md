[The Personal MBA](http://www.joshkaufman.net/personalmba/). John Kaufman says reading (**and practicing**) these 42 books should be as good as any MBA (and that an MBA is, perhaps excessively, expensive). Some of these books are worth a read in any case.

**Master Yourself**

- [Mastery](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0452267560/?tag=sanand-20) by George Leonard
- [Now, Discover Your Strengths](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0743201140/?tag=sanand-20) by Marcus Buckingham & Donald O. Clifton

**Manage Your Life and Work**

- [Getting Things Done](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0142000280/?tag=sanand-20) by David Allen
- [The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0743269519/?tag=sanand-20) by Stephen Covey

**Learn the Fundamentals**

- [What the CEO Wants You to Know](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0609608398/?tag=sanand-20) by Ram Charan
- [Profitable Growth Is Everyone's Business](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1400051525/?tag=sanand-20) by Ram Charan

**Strategic Thinking**

- [On Competition](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0875847951/?tag=sanand-20) by Michael Porter
- [Blue Ocean Strategy](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1591396190/?tag=sanand-20) by W. Chan Kim, Renee Mauborgne

**The Only Thing Constant is Change**

- [Seeing What's Next](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1591391857/?tag=sanand-20) by Clayton M. Christensen, Erik A. Roth, Scott D. Anthony
- [Re-imagine](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/078949647X/?tag=sanand-20) by Tom Peters

**Masters of Management**

- [The Essential Drucker](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/006093574X/?tag=sanand-20) by Peter Drucker
- [First, Break All the Rules](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0684852861/?tag=sanand-20) by Marcus Buckingham & Curt Coffman

**The Finest Minds in Business**

- [The Essays of Warren Buffett](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0966446119/?tag=sanand-20) by Warren Buffett & Lawrence Cunningham
- [Poor Charlie's Almanack](http://www.poorcharliesalmanack.com/) by Charlie Munger & Peter Kaufman

**Dollars on the Books**

- [The McGraw-Hill 36-Hour Course in Finance for Nonfinancial Managers](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0071425462/?tag=sanand-20) by Robert A. Cooke
- [Essentials of Accounting, 8th edition](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0131025716/?tag=sanand-20) by Robert Anthony, Leslie Pearlman

**Numbers and Negotiations**

- [How to Read a Financial Report](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0471478679/?tag=sanand-20) by John A. Tracy
- [Getting To Yes](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0140157352/?tag=sanand-20) by Roger Fisher, William Ury, and Bruce Patton

**Operational Effectiveness**

- [The Goal](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0884271781/?tag=sanand-20) by Eliyahu Goldratt & Jeff Cox
- [Lean Thinking](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0743249275/?tag=sanand-20) by James Womack & Daniel Jones

**Form and Function**

- [The Substance of Style](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0060186321/?tag=sanand-20) by Virginia Postrel
- [The Design of Everyday Things](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0465067107/?tag=sanand-20) by Donald A. Norman

**Project Management and Marketing**

- [The Art of Project Management](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0596007868/?tag=sanand-20) by Scott Berkun
- [The Marketing Playbook](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1591840384/?tag=sanand-20) by John Zagula & Richard Tong

**Do Your Own Thing**

- [The Art of the Start](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1591840562/?tag=sanand-20) by Guy Kawasaki
- [The Bootstrapper's Bible](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00005R2F8/?tag=sanand-20) by Seth Godin

**Speak Your Mind**

- [On Writing Well](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0060006641/?tag=sanand-20) by William Zinsser
- [Flawless Consulting](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0787948039/?tag=sanand-20) by Peter Block

**The Delicate Art of Human Relations**

- [How To Win Friends and Influence People](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0671723650/?tag=sanand-20) by Dale Carnegie
- [Crucial Conversations](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0071401946/?tag=sanand-20) by Kerry Patterson, Joseph Grenny, Ron McMillan, and Al Switzler

**Sell, Sell, Sell!**

- [The Little Red Book of Selling](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1885167601/?tag=sanand-20) by Jeffrey Gitomer
- [Influence: The Psychology of Persuasion](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0688128165/?tag=sanand-20) by Robert B. Cialdini

**Economics and Worldviews**

- [Economics in One Lesson](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0517548232/?tag=sanand-20) by Henry Hazlitt
- [The Economist](http://www.economist.com/)

**Businesses, Past and Present**

- [American Business, 1920-2000](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0882959859/?tag=sanand-20) by Thomas McCraw, John Franklin, A. S. Eisenstadt
- [Brand New](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/1578512212/?tag=sanand-20) by Nancy F. Koehn

**Rules and Morals**

- [Law 101](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0195132653/?tag=sanand-20) by Jay M. Feinman
- [A Primer on Business Ethics](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0742513890/?tag=sanand-20) by Tibor Machan & James Chesher

**Analyze This**

- [The 80/20 Principle](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0385491743/?tag=sanand-20) by Richard Koch
- [Principles of Statistics](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0486637603/?tag=sanand-20) by M.G. Bulmer

**Voices of Experience**

- [The Little Book of Business Wisdom](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/0471369799/?tag=sanand-20) by Peter Krass (Editor)
- [Swanson's Unwritten Rules of Management](http://wwwxt.raytheon.com/communications/whs_rules/) by Bill Swanson
